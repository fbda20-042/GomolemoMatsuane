HISTORY OF LAS VEGAS

The name Las Vegas means "meadows" in Spanish ,this city was named Las Vegas by Rafael Rivera, a
member of the Spanish explorer Antonio Armijo trading party who was travelling to Los Angeles and
stopped for water there on the Old Spanish Trail.
Las Vegas was foundedin 1905 after the opening of a rail road that li linked Los Angeles and Salt Lake City.
Las Vegas embrace of Old West style freedoms-provided a perfect home for East Coast organised crime.In
the beginning of 1940s money from drugs was used to build casinos and was laundered within them.
